Ratters- The new international money

14/09/2025

By Shy Moai


Since the creation of Narconcilia, every action the government took, every law the citizens suggested, every decision we took as a community have been based around one big goal : promoting Narconcilia from a micronation to a real nation. But what do we really need to make Narconcilia into a nation ? If we compare Narconcilia to other bigger nations, we can see that there are some concepts and system that Narconcilia do not have yet. Not only that, but if we want to become a nation, we need to function as a nation too. So now that we know that, what are the big priorities of Narconcilia at the moment and how do they contribute to making Narconcilia closer to a real nation ?

Recongnition from the UN

To be considered a "nation" or country, we basically only need to fill out one criteria. Be recognized as a country by all members of the United Nations. This means that the official governement countries that are part of the UN must consider Narconcilia as a country of its own. Now, to get recognition in the fiirst place there are a few must-have. Some of these must-have are achievable for Narconcilia in a close future and some other are a lot more difficult. And obviously, we can't talk about all of them in a single newspaper article. So here is the top 10 of these requirements, from hardly possible to easily achievable

  1. Army

    Ah yes, let's convince people to fight against a army of thousands of people that have tanks with only like a knife. Not a real requirement though, many real-life nations (like Vatican) don't have a functional army

  2. Healthcare and education

    How are we going to have a consistent education system if we have territories everywhere in the world. Also what on earth are we going to teach in history class ?? Healthcare has pretty much the same problem as army.

  3. Defined Borders

    Unless we have someone ready to negotiate for months with other countries to agree on where do our borders start and where do they end, this is going to be very tedious and difficult.

  4. Basic needs

    This includes all the things like access to clean water, access to electricity and access to housing. This is once again an issue because the Naroncilian territory is way too spread out. It will be difficult to have a sewage system for every embassy.

  5. Tax system

    Obviously, if we want to offer some basic services as a government you need to get funds. Now the real problem is how do we make a tax system so that the super rich can't evade taxes and how do we tax people's possesions that are outside of Narconcilia. So basically a lot of smaller problems for this.

  6. Functionning laws

    Yes, this could take a while to set up but I feel like if everyone helps a bit for the law writing, we can get this done, even if our law system might not be perfect at first, I'm pretty confident it can be functionning.

  7. Permanent residents

    These are people that live in Naroncilia, are bound by the laws of Narconcilia and live with Narconcilia system. We basically just need motivated and reasonable citizens and we're set.

  8. Functionning Economy

    Why is this so far down the list ? Because it will be implemented in Narconcilia in only a few months ! You will finally be able to pay your Naroncilian

  9. Governemnt

    Not only do we have a government, we have a more democratic one than the vast majority of current real nations.

  10. Citizens

    We already have those !

Naroncilian economy

This is what you came here for isn't it ?

In the last two government meetings, the major subject was to develop an economy system for Narconcilia. And even if the trade system hasn't been developped as deeply as the other aspects, it is one of those things that just creates itself. The currency would be ratters (ratts in french) and you could pay with special coins that would be produced by the N.I.E.T. The coins would be in plastic so they would be lighter and cheaper to produce than metal coins as well as being eaasily 3d printable. Each coin would be of a different shape to make it easier for people with disabilities to distinguish one coin from another. The edges of the coins are rounded to prevent possible injuries and inconvenience while using them. A list of the shapes and values of the current coin designs is available at the end of the article. The coins will first be a pilote project, being only available to people living in proximity of the N.I.E.T. production center because long distance transactions would be much harder to regulate and be much less efficient. Then, if after a few months, we see the economy system is used and works well, it would be expanded to the international. A bank managed by the N.I.E.T. would hold people's assets and could allow long distance transaction. Using a pre-determined password, a user could tranfer some of its coins to another user. The bank would then move the amount of coins from one account to the other. Users could deposit or withdraw coins at any given time by seeing a N.I.E.T. banker and asking for the operation to happen.

N.I.E.T.

The NIET or Naroncilian Institute of Economy and Trade (INEC in french for Institut Naroncilien de l'Économie et du Commerce) will be an organization that would be managed by a government member designed by a vote amongst all members. It would consist of any citizens that would like to participate in developping the Naroncilian economy. The members of the NIET would manage transactions via the bank, search for ways to improve our economy system and present those at government meetings. Basically specialists in the field of the economy so not 2 or 3 person do everythiing in the micronation. Other organizations will also probably soon be created to try and motivate people to help improve Narconcilia. If you want to design logos and flags, if you want to write newspaper articles, if you want to write laws or anything else, you will be able to do it with other people that are also interested in the same field. So if you are interested by any of these (NIET included), clearly state it either in a public chatroom of Naroncilia or privately dm Shy Moai if you dont want to share it with other citizens for some reason.

Narconcilia elections of June 2025 - What happened ?

15/06/2025

By Shy Moai


After not one, but two elections, Narconcilia finally has a prime minister and three ministers. Now, the plans, reactions and even the precise results of the elections were still not very clear. This is mainly due to the fact that the second election did not get as much attention as the first time. This is why Shy Moai asked everyone to write an article on the elections, even promising a pay to whoever wrote the article. Unfortunately, no article was submitted and not much was explained. Now, here is everything you need to know about the first elections of Narconcilia.

Why a Second Election ?

After the first election, when the results were compiled to find the winner, something weird was observed. 12 votes had been casted but only 11 people were Narconcilian citizens at the time. Additionally, 2 citizens said that they did not vote, thus meaning at least 3 votes in the 12 votes were not valid. The election was then cancelled and another one was triggered on June 8th. This time, the voters had to include their Narconcilian ID, an 11 character code that is unique to each citizen. That way, any form of cheating would be prevented.

The Results

On June 9th, the results were annouced : Kittanaaa won the first elections of Narconcilia. He then chose his ministers. Mr.Rat, Scrawny Motherfucker and Neon Sheep were chosen and became the first ministers of Narocncilia. 11 people out of the 11 citizens of Narconcilia voted this election, making the voter turnout 100%, which is extremely rare. But it is not that suprising as Narconcilia has a lot less citizens that other countries, making reaching a voter turnout of 100% much easier. Now, here is a deeper analysis of the election's results.

  • Kittanaaa was placed higher than Neon Sheep in 73% of ballots
  • Neon Sheep was placed higher than Shy Moai in 55% of ballots
  • Kittanaaa was placed higher than Shy Moai in 82% of ballots

What Now ?

When the two candidates were asked if they were going to present themselves as president next month, both said yes. We still do not know if other people will present themselves but after all, the next forced election is in two whole months so anything can still happen. It doesn't looks like the results of the election angered or triggered anybody so the possibility of an early vote next month is really low. Concerning the plans of Kittanaaa for the future, he said "I plan to revise laws, add laws, improve our economy, and generally try to help our community. I also plan to make our government more democratic.". Shy Moai also has a lot of plans for the future, including the addition of laws on the creation and modification of laws, on the functionning of government meetings as well as a potentially reliable economic system. Neon Sheep also doesn't give up on getting recognition from other micronations and plans to try to contact some other micronations soon.

With a real government, a way to communicate information to everyone and an invested community, Narconcilia is now getting closer and closer to a real nation. The great land of spinning rats is growing at an insane rate, going from 3 citizens and a few laws to 12 citizens, a website, a government and lots of ambitions. And now, with a lot more decisions to take, the government meetings should start soon and bring all the projects of the citizens to life.









How the Spinning Rat Meme was Born

09/06/2025

By Duck


It all began with a pixelized rodent and a hypnotic spin.In an age where digital trends come and go in the blink of an eye, the Spinning Rat meme has managed to carve out a peculiar corner of the internet. Both absurd and strangely endearing, this endlessly twirling rat has captured the imagination of millions; turning an unlikely rodent into a viral icon. But how exactly did this dizzy little creature spin its way into web history?

The Emergence of the Spinning Meme

The exact origin of this meme is a well hidden rabbit hole but since there was no starting point, as it evolved within the community over the time from far before the spinning meme like the spinning potato chips or the spinning rat meme appeared. However, several early examples and related memes have contributed to its development. In 2004, many videos about characters spinning on "You Spin Me Round (Like a Record)" by Dead or Alive soundtrack. Since then, the spinning and the looped music thing really started catching people's attention. In 2006, the Loituma Girl meme was released by Leekspin and is now right under the one million views milestone.

The Spinning Meme Explosion

On September 25th, 2020, the user @MrFatCatMan uploaded a GIF of a fish spinning while the music "Bumpy Ride, Nightcore Version" by Mohombi. This marked the beginning of the modern era of the spinning meme. During the months following this event, the meme became famous and many of the legendary memes as “spinning chips”, “spinning roach” and “spinning chair” were uploaded just after. In the years following the spinning fish release, the spinning meme accumulated from 150 millions to 250 millions views on the main social media platforms.

The Rise of the Spinning Rat: From Deus Ex to Meme Icon

On March 19th, 2020, a Fandom user known as @Hawkybird uploaded an unusual image to the Deus Ex Wiki. Released on the "Animals" page, the GIF of a low resolution rat from the 2000 video game Deus Ex, spinning on its axis with the caption: "A rotating image of a rat.", was firstly left out and forgotten. Months later, on November 24th, 2020, the surreal image resurfaced on Twitter. The gimmick account @LowPolyAnimals, known for posting pixelized and retro-styled animals, shared the GIF. The post quickly gained popularity, reaching over 2,000 retweets and nearly 14,000 likes in the span of two years. But it was in March 2021 that the rotating rodent fully entered meme lore. On March 29th, a 4chan user named @Munashe uploaded a 3D animation of a spinning low-poly rat. The animation spread rapidly across platforms, celebrated for its absurd simplicity. By August 24th, 2021, it had reached Tenor's GIF library thanks to user @doseshipment, ensuring its place in countless text threads and meme compilations.

The Spinning rat meme

While horizontal spinning rat memes was at its peak in 2021, some upload of rat spinning vertically, diagonally, in 4k quality or with no more than 8 pixels were released. Dedicated users were trying to add their absurd variant of spinning rat spinning in their own way to the spinning rat monument. On November 9 of 2023, @twotwelve, an independent programming team, almost immediately gathered dozens of spinning rats lovers on their new website, spinningrat.online .
