Narconcilia elections of June 2025 - What happened ?

15/06/2025

By Shy Moai


After not one, but two elections, Narconcilia finally has a prime minister and three ministers. Now, the plans, reactions and even the precise results of the elections were still not very clear. This is mainly due to the fact that the second election did not get as much attention as the first time. This is why Shy Moai asked everyone to write an article on the elections, even promising a pay to whoever wrote the article. Unfortunately, no article was submitted and not much was explained. Now, here is everything you need to know about the first elections of Narconcilia.

Why a Second Election ?

After the first election, when the results were compiled to find the winner, something weird was observed. 12 votes had been casted but only 11 people were Narconcilian citizens at the time. Additionally, 2 citizens said that they did not vote, thus meaning at least 3 votes in the 12 votes were not valid. The election was then cancelled and another one was triggered on June 8th. This time, the voters had to include their Narconcilian ID, an 11 character code that is unique to each citizen. That way, any form of cheating would be prevented.

The Results

On June 9th, the results were annouced : Kittanaaa won the first elections of Narconcilia. He then chose his ministers. Mr.Rat, Scrawny Motherfucker and Neon Sheep were chosen and became the first ministers of Narocncilia. 11 people out of the 11 citizens of Narconcilia voted this election, making the vote turnout 100%, which is extremely rare. But it is not that suprising as Narconcilia has a lot less citizens that other countries, making reaching a vote turnout of 100% much easier. Now, here is a deeper analysis of the election's results.

  • Kittanaaa was placed higher than Neon Sheep in 73% of ballots
  • Neon Sheep was placed higher than Shy Moai in 55% of ballots
  • Kittanaaa was placed higher than Shy Moai in 82% of ballots

What Now ?

When the two candidates were asked if they were going to present themselves as president next month, both said yes. We still do not know if other people will present themselves but after all, the next forced election is in two whole months so anything can still happen. It doesn't looks like the results of the election angered or triggered anybody so the possibility of an early vote next month is really low. Concerning the plans of Kittanaaa for the future, he said "I plan to revise laws, add laws, improve our economy, and generally try to help our community. I also plan to make our government more democratic.". Shy Moai also has a lot of plans for the future, including the addition of laws on the creation and modification of laws, on the functionning of government meetings as well as a potentially reliable economic system. Neon Sheep also doesn't give up on getting recognition from other micronations and plans to try to contact some other micronations soon.

With a real government, a way to communicate information to everyone and an invested community, Narconcilia is now getting closer and closer to a real nation. The great land of spinning rats is growing at an insane rate, going from 3 citizens and a few laws to 12 citizens, a website, a government and lots of ambitions. And now, with a lot more decisions to take, the government meetings should start soon and bring all the projects of the citizens to life.

How the Spinning Rat Meme was Born

09/06/2025

By Duck


It all began with a pixelized rodent and a hypnotic spin.In an age where digital trends come and go in the blink of an eye, the Spinning Rat meme has managed to carve out a peculiar corner of the internet. Both absurd and strangely endearing, this endlessly twirling rat has captured the imagination of millions; turning an unlikely rodent into a viral icon. But how exactly did this dizzy little creature spin its way into web history?

The Emergence of the Spinning Meme

The exact origin of this meme is a well hidden rabbit hole but since there was no starting point, as it evolved within the community over the time from far before the spinning meme like the spinning potato chips or the spinning rat meme appeared. However, several early examples and related memes have contributed to its development. In 2004, many videos about characters spinning on "You Spin Me Round (Like a Record)" by Dead or Alive soundtrack. Since then, the spinning and the looped music thing really started catching people's attention. In 2006, the Loituma Girl meme was released by Leekspin and is now right under the one million views milestone.

The Spinning Meme Explosion

On September 25th, 2020, the user @MrFatCatMan uploaded a GIF of a fish spinning while the music "Bumpy Ride, Nightcore Version" by Mohombi. This marked the beginning of the modern era of the spinning meme. During the months following this event, the meme became famous and many of the legendary memes as “spinning chips”, “spinning roach” and “spinning chair” were uploaded just after. In the years following the spinning fish release, the spinning meme accumulated from 150 millions to 250 millions views on the main social media platforms.

The Rise of the Spinning Rat: From Deus Ex to Meme Icon

On March 19th, 2020, a Fandom user known as @Hawkybird uploaded an unusual image to the Deus Ex Wiki. Released on the "Animals" page, the GIF of a low resolution rat from the 2000 video game Deus Ex, spinning on its axis with the caption: "A rotating image of a rat.", was firstly left out and forgotten. Months later, on November 24th, 2020, the surreal image resurfaced on Twitter. The gimmick account @LowPolyAnimals, known for posting pixelized and retro-styled animals, shared the GIF. The post quickly gained popularity, reaching over 2,000 retweets and nearly 14,000 likes in the span of two years. But it was in March 2021 that the rotating rodent fully entered meme lore. On March 29th, a 4chan user named @Munashe uploaded a 3D animation of a spinning low-poly rat. The animation spread rapidly across platforms, celebrated for its absurd simplicity. By August 24th, 2021, it had reached Tenor's GIF library thanks to user @doseshipment, ensuring its place in countless text threads and meme compilations.

The Spinning rat meme

While horizontal spinning rat memes was at its peak in 2021, some upload of rat spinning vertically, diagonally, in 4k quality or with no more than 8 pixels were released. Dedicated users were trying to add their absurd variant of spinning rat spinning in their own way to the spinning rat monument. On November 9 of 2023, @twotwelve, an independent programming team, almost immediately gathered dozens of spinning rats lovers on their new website, spinningrat.online .

By Shy Moai

Kittanaaa

Kittanaaa is a member of the discord since April 7th 2025 and became a Narconcilian citizen on April 20th 2025. Since then, he has been very invested in both the micronation and the community. He is one of the fastest people to reach level 20, reaching it in only 11 days. As soon as he got his citizenship, he read all the laws and corrected a lot of mistakes Shy Moai made when he wrote the laws for example, mistranslations or continuity errors. He is also very invested in the events and in the economy aspect of the Micronation, almost studying all the possible ways to make money and their benefits. He is currently at more than a month or rat time and is still on spinningrat.online. Now, here is a short summary of his campaign and his promises :

  • Correcting all the errors in the laws documents
  • Creating a bot for the auctions system

Neon Sheep

Neon Sheep joined the discord serve on October 5th 2024 and became a citizen of Narconcilia on May 4th 2025. He is devoted to spinning rats, not only in the discord where he is level 5 but also everywhere else. He is an active promoter of Narconcilia and the discord in many other discord servers and other places. He created this embassy before even becoming a citizen, but it still got recognized as an official embassy by Shy Moai at the time. Neon Sheep currently has 5 minutes of rat time and unfortunately isn't still on spinningrat.online. Once again, here is a brief summary of his campaign and promises :

  • Bring movie night once per month
  • Getting recognized by other micronations and nations

Because with a population of only 10 citizens, your vote truly matters.
