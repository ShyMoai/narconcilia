How the Spinning Rat Meme was Born

09/06/2025

By Duck


It all began with a pixelized rodent and a hypnotic spin.In an age where digital trends come and go in the blink of an eye, the Spinning Rat meme has managed to carve out a peculiar corner of the internet. Both absurd and strangely endearing, this endlessly twirling rat has captured the imagination of millions; turning an unlikely rodent into a viral icon. But how exactly did this dizzy little creature spin its way into web history?

The Emergence of the Spinning Meme

The exact origin of this meme is a well hidden rabbit hole but since there was no starting point, as it evolved within the community over the time from far before the spinning meme like the spinning potato chips or the spinning rat meme appeared. However, several early examples and related memes have contributed to its development. In 2004, many videos about characters spinning on "You Spin Me Round (Like a Record)" by Dead or Alive soundtrack. Since then, the spinning and the looped music thing really started catching people's attention. In 2006, the Loituma Girl meme was released by Leekspin and is now right under the one million views milestone.

The Spinning Meme Explosion

On September 25th, 2020, the user @MrFatCatMan uploaded a GIF of a fish spinning while the music "Bumpy Ride, Nightcore Version" by Mohombi. This marked the beginning of the modern era of the spinning meme. During the months following this event, the meme became famous and many of the legendary memes as “spinning chips”, “spinning roach” and “spinning chair” were uploaded just after. In the years following the spinning fish release, the spinning meme accumulated from 150 millions to 250 millions views on the main social media platforms.

The Rise of the Spinning Rat: From Deus Ex to Meme Icon

On March 19th, 2020, a Fandom user known as @Hawkybird uploaded an unusual image to the Deus Ex Wiki. Released on the "Animals" page, the GIF of a low resolution rat from the 2000 video game Deus Ex, spinning on its axis with the caption: "A rotating image of a rat.", was firstly left out and forgotten. Months later, on November 24th, 2020, the surreal image resurfaced on Twitter. The gimmick account @LowPolyAnimals, known for posting pixelized and retro-styled animals, shared the GIF. The post quickly gained popularity, reaching over 2,000 retweets and nearly 14,000 likes in the span of two years. But it was in March 2021 that the rotating rodent fully entered meme lore. On March 29th, a 4chan user named @Munashe uploaded a 3D animation of a spinning low-poly rat. The animation spread rapidly across platforms, celebrated for its absurd simplicity. By August 24th, 2021, it had reached Tenor's GIF library thanks to user @doseshipment, ensuring its place in countless text threads and meme compilations.

The Spinning Rat Meme Variations

While horizontal spinning rat memes was at its peak in 2021, some upload of rat spinning vertically, diagonally, in 4k quality or with no more than 8 pixels were released. Dedicated users were trying to add their absurd variant of spinning rat spinning in their own way to the spinning rat monument. On November 9 of 2023, @twotwelve, an independent programming team, almost immediately gathered dozens of spinning rats lovers on their new website, spinningrat.online .
